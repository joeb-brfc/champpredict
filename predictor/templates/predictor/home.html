What is ChampPredict?

ChampPredict is a football prediction platform focused entirely on the English Championship. Users can submit score predictions for upcoming fixtures and earn points based on accuracy.

Unlike many prediction apps, ChampPredict is built specifically around the Championship, giving fans a dedicated space to follow and engage with one of the most competitive leagues.
